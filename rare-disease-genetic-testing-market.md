Fluorescence in Situ Hybridization (FISH) visualizes chromosomal abnormalities. Sanger Sequencing provides accurate sequencing for individual genes, while Karyotyping examines chromosomal structure. Additional methods may include newer technologies and specialized assays for rare diseases.</p></p>
