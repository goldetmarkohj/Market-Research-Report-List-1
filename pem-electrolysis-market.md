<p><p>PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolysis is a technology used for hydrogen production by splitting water into hydrogen and oxygen using electricity. This method employs a solid polymer electrolyte to conduct protons from the anode to the cathode while blocking electrons, enabling high efficiency and rapid response to fluctuations in power supply. The growing emphasis on renewable energy sources and the shift towards sustainable fuel alternatives are driving the adoption of PEM electrolysis.</p><p>The PEM Electrolysis Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period.
