<p><p>Search Engine Marketing Solutions encompass various pricing models tailored to different advertising goals. CPT (Cost Per Time) charges advertisers based on the duration of ad visibility. CPA (Cost-Per-Acquisition) focuses on the cost for each customer acquired through the ad. CPC (Cost-Per-Click) bills advertisers for each click generated. CPM (Cost-Per-Thousand-Impressions) sets a price for every thousand ad views. Other models may include cost-per-lead or fixed-price contracts, each catering to specific marketing strategies and performance metrics.</p></p>
