<p><p>The Portable Spectrum Analyzer market is primarily divided into two types: Frequency Sweep Type Spectrum Analyzers and Real-Time Spectrum Analyzers. Frequency Sweep Type Spectrum Analyzers measure signal frequencies over time, capturing data in a sequential manner, making them effective for identifying signal characteristics. In contrast, Real-Time Spectrum Analyzers continuously monitor and display frequency data, allowing for immediate detection of transient signals and dynamic frequency changes. Both types cater to various applications in telecommunications, electronics testing, and signal monitoring.</p></p>
