<p><p>The commercial charbroilers market primarily consists of gas charbroilers and electric charbroilers. Gas charbroilers use natural gas or propane, offering quick heating and high-temperature cooking, making them ideal for fast-paced kitchens. They are often preferred for their traditional grilling flavor and efficiency. Electric charbroilers, on the other hand, operate using electricity, providing more consistent heat and easier temperature control.
