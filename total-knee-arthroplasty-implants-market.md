<p><p>The Total Knee Arthroplasty (TKA) implants market consists of two primary types: fixed-bearing implants and mobile-bearing implants. Fixed-bearing implants feature a stationary polyethylene component that offers stability and durability, making them suitable for many patients. In contrast, mobile-bearing implants have a movable polyethylene insert that enhances range of motion and may reduce wear, potentially benefiting younger, more active patients. Both types are designed to relieve pain and restore function, addressing the growing demand for knee replacement procedures.</p></p>
