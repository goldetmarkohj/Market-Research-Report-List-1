<p><p>The smoke extraction fan market is primarily categorized into centrifugal, axial, and other types. Centrifugal fans are designed to move air and smoke efficiently through ducts or vents, utilizing a high-pressure design for improved airflow in larger spaces. Axial fans, on the other hand, directly push air in a straight line, making them suitable for applications requiring high volume and lower pressure. Other types may include specialized designs, such as jet fans or tube axial fans, tailored for specific smoke control needs.</p></p>
