<p><p>The Non-Operative Spine Care market encompasses various treatment modalities for managing spinal pain without surgery. Spinal injections, including epidural injections, deliver anti-inflammatory medications directly to affected areas. Facet joint injections target pain from joint inflammation, while sacroiliac joint injections relieve discomfort in the lower back. Provocation discography assesses discogenic pain by injecting contrast dye into discs. Other treatments may include physical therapy, chiropractic adjustments, and medication management, all aimed at reducing pain and improving mobility while avoiding surgical intervention.</p></p>
